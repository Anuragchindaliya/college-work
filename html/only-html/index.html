About
Historical Perspective
Created more facilities for students

Pt. Jawahar Lal Nehru Government College, Faridabad is one of the oldest and the most distinguished college in the region. Established in 1971, the college is situated in Sector 16 A, in the heart of the city. Spread out in a sprawling campus of 12.5 Acres, the college boasts of proud history, with national and international achievements in Sports, Cultural Activities, and National Service Scheme. The college has on its roll of honor many distinguished alumni, beginning with Hon'ble Sh. Krishan Pal Gurjar, the present Minister of State for Social Justice and Empowerment in Government of India. As a Member of Parliament in the Lok Sabha, he represents the Faridabad constituency in the state of Haryana. The college has given cricketers like Vijay Yadav, Athletes like Ajay Ratra, International Wrestlers like Neha Rathi, Parkour miracle Ziley Mawai and Popular Bollywood Singers like Himani Kapoor. Dr. Rakesh Gupta, Director, Sarvodaya Group of Hospitals, too has been a product of this college and has brought fame and glory in the domain of health services in the region.













VISION

 To be a centre of holistic education for participatory intellectual, creative, cultural and socio-political growth and development of the students.

MISSION
  • To help students grow into individuals of substance, with a sense of dignity and work ethic.
  • To facilitate development of humane outlook and responsible citizenship.
  • To promote care and concern for plants and animals, and our environment as a whole.
  • To combine career consciousness and livelihood goals with entrepreneurship, enterprise and community spirit.

The college has been a centre of excellence, and has received extensive patronage from the state government throughout. At present, the morning and evening shifts of the college are serving more than 6000 students. Being a centre of IGNOU, New Delhi, the college is an important hub of distance education as well.

The new building of the college is currently under construction, with state-of-the-art facilities. At present also, the college is one of the four colleges in the whole state, with a start-up incubator. Moreover, the college has some of the most qualified faculty in the whole state, with research experience at national and international levels. Academics along with Skill Development and Trainings, to improve our excellent placement programme is under a renewed focus. The college has always played a leading role in NSS, Youth Red Cross, Red Ribbon, Women Studies and Development Cell, NCC Army & Navy, and other co-curricular activities involving community outreach and has established a long-standing tradition of give and take with people of the city and surrounding areas.










The college logo सिद्धि: भवति कर्मजा i.e. Excellence through Diligence is our guiding principle, and inspires us towards consistent hard work and pursuit of excellence.
